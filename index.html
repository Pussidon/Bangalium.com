In here we will take part in experimentations. We will mainly focus in the feild of Physics. The first experiment is going to be about Oscillation. So, one may ask, what is Oscillation? well In Physics, oscillation means a repetitive motion that happens in a variation with respect to time. There is usually a central value, where the object would be at rest. Additionally, there are two or more positions between which the repetitive motion takes place. The prime example of oscillation is The tides in the sea and the movement of a simple pendulum of the clock are some of the most common examples of oscillations. The vibrations caused by the guitar strings and the other instruments having strings are also examples of oscillations.

Even the beating of our heart creates oscillations. Now, we will dive into pendulum. What is a pendulum?
